Privacy Policy – Candy Pop Quest

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected and used inside the game Candy Pop Quest.

1. Information We Collect

The game may collect non-personal data only, including:

  • Usage statistics through Firebase Analytics.
  • Non-personal advertising identifiers through Google AdMob.
  • Crash reports and technical diagnostics (Crashlytics).

No personal information (such as name, email, phone number, address, or precise location) is collected.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This game uses Google AdMob to display ads.

  • Google may collect non-personal data to improve ad quality.
  • Users can choose to opt-out of personalized ads through the in-game consent screen.
  • If the user denies consent, only non-personalized ads (NPA) are shown.

3. Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

Firebase Analytics helps us understand how players use the game.

  • Analytics is disabled automatically if the user denies consent.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties except:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads
  • Firebase / Crashlytics – for app analytics and crash reporting

These services handle data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and external services is encrypted using secure protocols.

We use standard security practices to protect data and prevent unauthorized access.

6. User Rights

Players can:

  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Disable analytics collection at any time through the consent screen (if provided)

7. Children’s Privacy

This game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary. Updates will be posted on this page.
